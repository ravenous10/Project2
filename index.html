Water Lily

This beaut is the Wanvisa, a Water Lilies favorite. It's a vigorous grower and has vibrant blooms that will sometimes turn out with yellow patches in the flower (yellow patches not pictured). Along with the variegated lily pads, this lily makes for one of the nicest hardy water lilies.

Use water lilies for the center, and shelf plants for the perimeter. Water lilies average 2 feet in length from the tuber to the lily pad. The minimum water depth required is 8 inches. The quantity of blooms and pads varies per plant, but on average there is 1-2 flowers and 5-7 lily pads.
